Leadership in Scouting

The scouting program has been an integral part of Samuel's life. You can explore Samuel's scout involvement and leadership on this page, starting from when Samuel joined the organization until the present time.

Order of the Arrow (OA)

The Order of the Arrow is the National Honor Society in the Boy Scouts of America. Scouts are recognized as members by peers in their scout Troop based on their living the values of the Scout Oath and Scout Law in their everyday lives.

Joined the OA: August 5th, 2017

Chapter Unit Elections Chair

Samuel began his first leadership position on the local level in 2018. In this position, Samuel coordinated the election of new members into the organization with the Chapter Chief, Scoutmasters, and other key stakeholders. He also hosted a Unit Elections training for other members of the chapter to instruct them in how to conduct Unit Elections.

December 2018 - December 2019

Wewoapisak Chapter Chief

Samuel started his first term as Chapter Chief in 2019. In this position, he oversaw fellow chapter leaders. He was responsible for activity planning, meeting schedules, unit elections, and communication with chapter members.

December 2019 - December 2020

Kithane Chapter Chief

The Middle Tennessee Council implemented a District and Chapter reorganization plan when Samuel began his second term as Chapter Chief. During this term, Samuel increased meeting membership for the new chapter from an average of 2-3 attendees to about 10-15 at each meeting and each activity.
