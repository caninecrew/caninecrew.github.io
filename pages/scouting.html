Eagle Scout Project

Masks for the Community

Completed: February 28, 2021 Mt. Juliet, TN

For my Eagle Scout project, I led a team of volunteers to produce and distribute over 500 handmade face masks to local schools and community members during the COVID-19 pandemic. This project involved coordinating volunteers, managing materials, and ensuring quality control to provide essential protective equipment to those in need.
