What Scouting Means to Me

Service

Giving back to the community is at the core of Scouting. Through hundreds of hours of service, I've learned the value of stewardship and helping others.

Preparedness

Being prepared isn't just a motto; it's a mindset. Scouting taught me to anticipate challenges and develop the skills to handle them effectively.

Leadership

Leading peers is one of the toughest challenges. My journey gave me a safe environment to fail, learn, and grow into a confident leader.

Eagle Scout Project: Masks for the Community

Completed: February 28, 2021 Mt. Juliet, TN

For my Eagle Scout project, I led a team of volunteers to produce and distribute over 500 handmade face masks to local schools and community members during the COVID-19 pandemic. This project involved coordinating volunteers, managing materials, and ensuring quality control to provide essential protective equipment to those in need.
