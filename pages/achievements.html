Achievements

  • James E. West Fellowship
    Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge, Order of the Arrow
    Issued Dec 2024
    The James E. West Fellowship was established in 1994 to acknowledge Scouters in the Middle Tennessee area that donated $1000.00 or more to the Council’s Endowment. The Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge has provided monetary donations to fund several James E. West Fellowship Awards. The Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge presents these Fellowships to Arrowmen who have spent countless hours furthering the ideals of the Order of the Arrow. This award is given as appropriate.
    James E. West Fellowship
  • Tom Parker Memorial Award
    Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge, Order of the Arrow
    Issued Dec 2024
    The Tom Parker Memorial Award for Excellence in Camp Promotion was originally given to recognize those who had demonstrated excellence in promoting camping opportunities on the district and council level. In 2010, the award was broadened to include those who have best demonstrated the spirit of the Order while serving on the Middle Tennessee Council’s summer camp staff.
