James E. West Fellowship

Dec 2024

Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge, Order of the Arrow

The James E. West Fellowship was established in 1994 to acknowledge Scouters in the Middle Tennessee area that donated $1000.00 or more to the Council's Endowment. The Wa-Hi-Nasa Lodge presents these Fellowships to Arrowmen who have spent countless hours furthering the ideals of the Order of the Arrow.
